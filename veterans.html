Veterans Program

Each month, DSG Exterior Cleaning provides one complimentary exterior cleaning job to a veteran in our service area. Nominations include a short story and basic property details so we can confirm fit, access, and safety.

Note: selection, scheduling, and scope depend on access and safety considerations.

How it works

1) Nominate a veteran (self, family, or community nomination).

2) Review for safety, access, and scope fit.

3) Schedule the service at a mutually workable time.

Recommended nomination details:

  • Veteran name + town
  • Short story (why the service matters)
  • Property type and service needed
  • Any access concerns (height, steep grades, etc.)
