About DSG Exterior Cleaning

Professional care for your home

Our Story

DSG Exterior Cleaning was founded with a simple mission: provide professional, reliable exterior cleaning services to homeowners across the Hudson Valley. We believe your home deserves the best care, using professional equipment and proven techniques.

Our Commitment

We're committed to delivering exceptional results with every job. That means using low-pressure soft wash methods that safely clean without damage, protecting your landscaping, and leaving your property looking better than when we arrived.

Why Choose DSG?

Professional Equipment: We invest in commercial-grade equipment to deliver results DIY methods can't match.

Trained Technicians: Our team is trained in proper cleaning techniques for different surfaces and materials.

Licensed & Insured: Fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind.

Community Focused: Proud to serve our Hudson Valley community, including our Veterans Program honoring those who served.

Service Areas

We proudly serve Westchester, Putnam, and Dutchess Counties, including Bedford, Mahopac, Millbrook, Rhinebeck, and 40+ other Hudson Valley communities.
