About DSG Exterior Cleaning

DSG Exterior Cleaning was built on a simple idea: exterior cleaning should protect your home, not put it at risk.

Our Philosophy

Many exterior cleaning companies focus on speed and volume. We take a different approach. Every surface, material, and home is treated individually — with the right method, pressure, and products for the job.

That means soft washing where low pressure is required, pressure washing only where appropriate, and professional window and gutter cleaning performed with safety and care.

Professional, Safety-First Methods

Our technicians follow clear procedures designed to protect siding, roofing, landscaping, and surrounding property. We do not cut corners, rush jobs, or use excessive pressure.

This approach leads to better results, longer-lasting cleanliness, and peace of mind for homeowners.

Serving the Hudson Valley

DSG Exterior Cleaning proudly serves homeowners across Dutchess County, Putnam County, and Upper Westchester.

Whether you’re maintaining your home, preparing to sell, or simply want professional exterior care, we’re here to help.

Giving Back

As part of our commitment to the community, DSG Exterior Cleaning provides one complimentary exterior cleaning job per month to a veteran in our service area.

This program reflects our belief that professional service should extend beyond business and into the community we serve.
